Residential Development

Landmark EPC partners with residential developers to provide engineering and construction services. Our depth of experience and services allows us to take projects from conception to completion. Landmark takes a construction-driven engineering approach to our residential projects. By getting all phases of the project working together from the design phase, we greatly reduce errors and additional cost for our clients.
